My interests:

I'm interested in data science because I've always been fascinated in learning how things work and understanding why things are the way they are.

As a child I enjoyed taking apart my toys to see how the individual pieces were put together and I was also a big bug collector as I enjoyed observing their interactions with one another. I think data science is just another medium that allows me to express my curiosity as now I can use data to better understand the world.

When I'm not engrossed in front of a computer screen, I enjoy activities such as being outdoors in nature, kickboxing, learning about sustainable health.

My values:

Continuous learning and growth, self discipline, vitality.
